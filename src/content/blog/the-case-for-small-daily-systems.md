Most personal goals fail from operational complexity, not lack of intent.

If a routine depends on perfect mood, perfect timing, and perfect energy, it is not a routine. It is a gamble.

I now design personal systems with the same constraints I use for production systems:

- low setup cost,
- clear trigger,
- obvious completion state,
- forgiving failure recovery.

The result is less drama and more progress. Small systems are not glamorous, but they survive real life.
